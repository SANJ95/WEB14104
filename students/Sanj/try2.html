Family Apple Pie Recipe

By Sanj Pardesi

Prep Time and information

  • Prep time: 30 min Cook time: 1 hour

    • Total time: 1 hr 30 min Yield: 1 9" pie (8 servings) Serving size: 1 medium slice

    Calories per serving: 250 Fat per serving: 12g Ingredients: Thinly-sliced apples: 6 cups White sugar: 3/4 cup

    Directions:

    1. Cut and peel apples

    2. Mix sugar and cinnamon. Use additional sugar for tart apples.

    3. add a spoon of nutella.

    4.Mix it with flour for 3 minutes

    5. mix anymore chocolate you want to (0.15ml)

    6. Then after let it settle and put in the oven.

    Ingredients

    1. Nuttela

    2. Eggs

    3. Milk

    4. Apple (thinly sliced apples)

    5. White Sugar: 3/4 Cups

    6. any type of syrup you want to add, strawberry, chocolate,banana, honey etc

    7. Just mix 0.50ml with the slided apples!

    8. Baking powder

    9. Self raising flour

    10. caster sugar

    11. Cream (to pour on apple pie once finished)
